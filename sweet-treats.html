Fudgy Brownie Bliss

This dessert is so good it’s sinful, but don't worry — we won’t fudge the details!

It all starts with a freshly baked brownie that is absolute fudge-ation, topped off by a creamy scoop of pure vanilla. Just when you thought it couldn’t get any cooler, it is drizzled in rich chocolate syrup and topped with whipped cream and a cherry. It’s truly the cherry on tp of a sweet deal!



A Match-a Made in Heaven?

This elegant par-fait is strictly for the sophisticated palate. Featuring a deeply rich, slightly-bitter matcha ice cream, paired with chewy mochi, red bean paste, and a matcha wafer roll. It’s a dessert that has truly reached peak matcha-rity.

Not ready to grow up yet? If your taste buds are still a little immature, don’t throw a tantrum! We can swap it out for our sweeter matcha ice cream so you can keep living in blissful, sugary denial.
