What Makes Our Scoops Special

100% Fresh

We don’t do shortcuts. Our dairy is sourced from local pasture-raised farms, our vanilla beans are split and scraped by hand, and our mix-ins are baked right here in our kitchen. If it isn’t fresh, real, and premium, it doesn’t make it into our batch.

Local Love

For us, ice cream is more than just a dessert—it's an experience. We slow-churn our bases in small batches to ensure a velvety, melt-in-your-mouth texture. From the way we hand-roll our waffle cones to the final swirl of caramel, a genuine love for the craft goes into every single serving.

The Sweetest Spot in Town

We’re not just serving ice cream; we’re serving up fun! From weekly flavor-naming contests to holiday pop-up events, there is always something exciting happening at Ambrosial Delish. We believe every visit should feel like a mini-vacation and a guaranteed unforgettable experience.

The Big Bang of Flavour

Ambrosial Delish officially burst onto the scene in 2023, born from a late-night kitchen experiment that got delightfully out of hand. Armed with a temperamental home ice cream maker and a serious sugar craving, we accidentally created a slow-churned sweet cream base so velvety, a friend took one bite and declared it "purely ambrosial."

We outgrew that tiny kitchen instantly, but the wild energy remained. Two years later, we're still hand-crafting every batch, throwing spontaneous giveaways, and turning a simple scoop into a neighbourhood celebration.
